Who I am

I am a university student studying game development in the Bachelor of IT. Currently 2 years into my degree, I have been spending all of my time devoted to university and to creating games and demos to practice my skills and to learn as much as I can.

I spent most of my time programming, primarily in C++ and C#, though I have learned how to work with Java when required. I have also done numerous classes learning how to work with Maya and develop aesthetic 3D models. Alongside this I have learned how to make accurate models in Fusion360 to develop 3D models prime for 3D printing. As a result of all of this 3D modelling, I have also learned how to utilise Adobe Photoshop to create textures and other artworks for my products.
